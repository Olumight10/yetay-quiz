Our Story

The Vision Behind Yetay Bible Competition

Our Vision

Yetay is a divine initiative birthed to bring young minds together from various denominations through healthy Bible competition. We envision a generation deeply rooted in the Word, standing as light to the nations.

Our Mission

We promote biblical literacy, unity, and growth. Yetay serves as a platform where spiritual excellence meets friendly rivalry, where hearts burn for the Word, and where youths bond beyond doctrine.

The Story So Far

The journey began in 2024, under the Banner Unto Nations Foundation. What started as a vision for a local quiz has rapidly transformed into a landmark interdenominational event.

Today, Yetay draws participants from across various regions, breaking denominational barriers and fostering a community of young believers who are unashamed of the Gospel of Christ.
